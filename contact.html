Common Questions

What to expect when you engage us

What happens after I schedule a briefing?↓
You'll receive a calendar confirmation along with a short pre-briefing questionnaire. This allows us to arrive prepared. The session itself is 45 minutes — structured, substantive, and focused entirely on your context.
How quickly can you mobilise a team?↓
For scoped engagements, we typically mobilise within 1–2 weeks of agreement execution. For staff augmentation and embedded teams, we can often onboard within days, subject to security and onboarding requirements.
Do you work exclusively with large enterprises?↓
Our primary focus is enterprise and public-sector organisations. We also work with high-growth technology companies and government bodies at national and regional level. Engagements are scoped based on complexity and strategic fit, not company size alone.
What geographies do you operate in?↓
We operate across the MENA region with active delivery capability in Jordan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Iraq, and the Levant. We have successfully delivered projects for clients in 15+ countries, including North America and Europe.
Do you offer fixed-price or time-and-materials contracts?↓
Both models are available. Fixed-price contracts are recommended for well-defined programmes with stable requirements. Time-and-materials engagements suit iterative or evolving scope. We advise on the most appropriate commercial structure during the scoping phase.
What distinguishes ACT Plus as a transformation partner?↓
We combine deep regional expertise with internationally benchmarked delivery standards. Our leadership team holds long-term client relationships as a core operating principle — not a sales objective. We are accountable at every stage, and we measure success by the outcomes we enable, not the hours we bill.
