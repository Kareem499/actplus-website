Founded in 2019, ACT Plus Digital Solutions is a single, accountable delivery partner for enterprises across Palestine, Jordan, Iraq, the UAE and beyond — operating as one team across strategy, engineering, cybersecurity, and managed services.

Who We Are

One team. One accountable partner.

ACT Plus Digital Solutions was founded in 2019 in Ramallah, Palestine with a clear purpose: to give enterprises in the MENA region a single, trusted partner that could own the full digital transformation journey — not just one slice of it.

Too often, enterprises navigate a fragmented landscape of consultants, vendors, and system integrators who each own a piece but nobody owns the outcome. We built ACT Plus to be different — a firm where strategy, engineering, cybersecurity, and managed services operate as one unified practice under one roof.

Today we serve leading organizations across Palestine, Jordan, Iraq, the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and Canada — including national telecoms, central banks, ministries, international development organizations, and fast-growing private sector companies.
